Election 2018

What to watch for in Minnesota's legislative races this year

By Jeff Hargarten • Star Tribune

Control of Minnesota’s Legislature tends to blow with the up-ballot winds, due in large part to a couple dozen swing seats in greater Minnesota and the Twin Cities suburbs.

During presidential election years, the winner's party tends to ride those victorious coattails to a legislative majority. Inversely, midterms usually result in backlashes against the majority party and the pendulum swings back the other way.
